Recipe 3

Iced Tea

Description

Cool off with a refreshing glass of iced tea! This classic beverage is perfect for hot summer days or anytime you need a thirst-quenching drink. Enjoy it plain or with a slice of lemon for an extra burst of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea bags (black or green)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Remove from heat and add tea bags, allowing them to steep for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Remove tea bags and stir in sugar if desired.
  4. Let the tea cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until cold.
  5. Serve over ice and enjoy!
